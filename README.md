## Featured Projects

**[Silicon PN Junction — Temperature-Dependent TCAD Analysis](https://github.com/HassanEmad2006/Silicon-PN-Junction-Temperature-TCAD)**
Simulated an abrupt silicon p-n junction in Silvaco ATLAS across a 25 K–900 K sweep, characterizing carrier concentration through freeze-out, partial ionization, extrinsic, and intrinsic regions. Observed a non-monotonic built-in potential, peaking around 75–100 K before decreasing at higher temperatures, and benchmarked results against analytical carrier-statistics theory.

**[Semiconductor in Nanodevices](https://github.com/HassanEmad2006/Semiconductor-Nanodevices)**
Team project reviewing semiconductor materials (Si, GaAs, graphene, MoS₂) by mobility and band gap, and examining the FinFET fabrication sequence in relation to carrier transport and doping theory.

**[Self-Balancing Robot — State-Space Modeling](https://github.com/HassanEmad2006/Self-Balancing-Robot-Control-System)**
Team project modeling a self-balancing robot as an inverted pendulum. Contributed to state-space derivation, PID controller design, and stability analysis in MATLAB, validated against measured hardware behavior.

**[Vector Mathematics Module — Engineering Mathematics Toolkit](https://github.com/HassanEmad2006/Python-Engineering-Mathematics-Toolkit)**
Team project building a Python numerical-methods toolkit. Individually developed the Vector Mathematics module (magnitude, dot/cross product, angle calculations).

**[Load Cell Transducer — Signal Conditioning](https://github.com/HassanEmad2006/Load-Cell-Transducer-LTspice-Arduino)**
Team project deriving Wheatstone-bridge output equations, simulating and filtering the raw signal in LTspice, and implementing calibrated weight measurement on Arduino.

**[Electromagnetic Principles of Particle Accelerators](https://github.com/HassanEmad2006/EM-Particle-Accelerators)**
Team project reviewing the electromagnetic operating principles of cyclotron, betatron, and synchrotron accelerators.
