## 📌 Featured Projects

**[Silicon PN Junction — Temperature-Dependent TCAD Analysis](https://github.com/HassanEmad2006/Silicon-PN-Junction-Temperature-TCAD)**
Simulated an abrupt silicon p-n junction in Silvaco ATLAS across a 25 K–900 K sweep, characterizing carrier concentration through freeze-out, partial ionization, extrinsic, and intrinsic regions. Observed a non-monotonic built-in potential, peaking around 75–100 K before decreasing at higher temperatures, and validated simulation results against analytical semiconductor theory.

**[RTP Chamber Thermal Simulation — COMSOL Multiphysics](https://github.com/HassanEmad2006/RTP-Chamber-Thermal-Simulation-COMSOL)**
Team project modeling a Rapid Thermal Processing (RTP) chamber in COMSOL, coupling conductive and surface-to-surface radiative heat transfer to simulate IR-lamp heating of a silicon wafer from 300 K to ~1,487 K within 60 seconds, demonstrating that radiative heat transfer becomes dominant above 1000°C.

**[Semiconductor Nanodevices: Materials & Device Physics](https://github.com/HassanEmad2006/Semiconductor-Nanodevices)**
Team project reviewing semiconductor materials (Si, GaAs, graphene, MoS₂) by mobility and band gap, and examining the FinFET fabrication sequence in relation to carrier transport and doping theory.

**[Audio Amplifier Design — Two-Stage BJT Circuit](https://github.com/HassanEmad2006/Audio-Amplifier-BJT-Design)**
Team project designing a two-stage BJT audio amplifier (Common Emitter with degeneration + Emitter Follower buffer) from a 12V single supply, targeting a tunable voltage gain of 4–10 while driving an 8Ω load. Solved the loading-effect problem with a low-output-impedance buffer stage, achieved PVT-stable biasing through emitter degeneration, and validated the full cascaded design in LTspice.

**[Self-Balancing Robot — State-Space Modeling](https://github.com/HassanEmad2006/Self-Balancing-Robot-Control-System)**
Team project modeling a self-balancing robot as an inverted pendulum. Contributed to state-space derivation, PID controller design, and stability analysis in MATLAB, validated against measured hardware behavior.

**[Load Cell Transducer — Signal Conditioning](https://github.com/HassanEmad2006/Load-Cell-Transducer-LTspice-Arduino)**
Team project deriving Wheatstone-bridge output equations, simulating and filtering the raw signal in LTspice, and implementing calibrated weight measurement on Arduino.

**[Electromagnetic Principles of Particle Accelerators](https://github.com/HassanEmad2006/EM-Particle-Accelerators)**
Team project reviewing the electromagnetic operating principles of cyclotron, betatron, and synchrotron accelerators.

**[Face Recognition Using Eigenfaces — A Linear Algebra Perspective](https://github.com/HassanEmad2006/Face-Recognition-Eigenfaces-Linear-Algebra)**
Team project deriving the Eigenface (PCA) method for face recognition from covariance matrices and eigenvectors, comparing its performance with LDA (Fisherfaces) and Tensorface methods.

**[Elliptic Curve Cryptography](https://github.com/HassanEmad2006/Elliptic-Curve-Cryptography)**
Report on Elliptic Curve Cryptography covering the algebraic group formed by points on an elliptic curve, the discrete logarithm problem as a one-way function, and the Elliptic Curve Diffie-Hellman (ECDH) protocol for secure key exchange. Includes a case study on Bitcoin's use of the secp256k1 curve to derive public keys and sign transactions.

**[Vector Mathematics Module — Engineering Mathematics Toolkit](https://github.com/HassanEmad2006/Python-Engineering-Mathematics-Toolkit)**
Team project building a Python numerical-methods toolkit. Individually developed the Vector Mathematics module (magnitude, dot/cross product, angle calculations).

**[Feasibility Study for a Solar Panel Project](https://github.com/HassanEmad2006/Solar-Panel-Feasibility-Study)**
Techno-economic feasibility study for a solar panel sales and installation business in Egypt, covering customer segmentation, package pricing, delivery costs, and full direct/indirect cost classification.
